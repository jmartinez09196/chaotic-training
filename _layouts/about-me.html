About Me

Embarking on My Fitness Odyssey: A Personal Journey

Before my corporate job, my life was characterized by excessive partying, heavy drinking, and a reliance on medications. Diagnosed with ADHD, anxiety, depression, and Tourette’s syndrome, I found myself at a crossroads. The corporate grind seemed like an escape, but it wasn’t the life I envisioned.

Fed up with the cycle of medications, I took a leap into the unknown, leaving the corporate world and diving into an isolated mountain lifestyle. This decision wasn’t just a geographic change; it was a commitment to personal transformation. In the quietude of the mountains, I learned the power of self-reliance and independence.

Returning to South Florida, I faced the aftermath of a decade marked by excess weight and obesity. Medications were replaced by a newfound dedication to fitness and real food. This shift wasn’t just physical; it sparked a desire for knowledge.

I entered the academic arena with a GPA of 1.6, facing challenges that seemed insurmountable. However, my journey was far from over. I persevered, and my dedication led to acceptance into the University of Central Florida’s Department of Health Professions and Sciences, focusing on Exercise Physiology & Human Performance. Graduating with a 3.74 GPA became not just a personal victory but a testament to the transformative power of resilience.

During this educational journey, I birthed Chaotic Training, not just as a fitness venture but as a holistic approach to well-being. ADHD, anxiety, depression, and Tourette’s weren’t hindrances anymore; they became driving forces behind my specialized approach. Fitness became my medication, and education became my empowerment.

Today, with over 5 years of experience, I stand not just as a fitness professional but as someone who intimately understands the transformative power of movement and a healthy lifestyle. My journey wasn’t scripted; it was lived. I guide those facing neurodiverse challenges, low self-esteem, anxiety, or anyone stepping into the realm of fitness.

Join me at Chaotic Training, where your unique journey is not just understood; it’s celebrated. Together, we’ll sculpt a stronger, more resilient version of you, liberated from the constraints of the past.
